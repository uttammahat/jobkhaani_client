about us

JobKhaani is an online marketplace and comprehensive freelancing portal, first of its kind in Nepal, where people can connect, collaborate and get their work done, either as an employee or as a freelancer.

Jobkhaani has various prelisted categories, where you can either post your job on your budget and requirement as employer, or bid to complete posted jobs as freelancer. It’s fast, simple, and cost-effective solution to find and hire best talents anywhere, any time.
